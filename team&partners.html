Partners


Queens Health Society

The Queens Health Society is a non-profit organization who's mission is to raise awareness and educate thee Queens community about prominent health issues in today's society.

Young Israel of Sunnyside

The Young Israel of Sunnyside is a traditional synagogue in a charming neighborhood in Queens, just east of Manhattan, they contribute to our funding, as well as some cultural programs.

Melinda Katz President, Borough of Queens

This organization specifically deals with educational events in Queens led by Bourough President, Michelle Katz... they help us to fund some of our own educational programs.

The Queens Chamber of Commerce

As a major contributor to the QCC, their mission is to conribute to a vibrant and culturally diverse Queens by fostering connections, and educating.
